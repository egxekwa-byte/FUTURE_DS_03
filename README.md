**Data Science & Analytics – Task 3 (2026)**
By Future Interns

---

## 🔍 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing marketing funnel and lead data to understand how users progress from leads to customers. The goal is to identify conversion patterns, highlight drop-off points, and provide actionable insights that can improve business performance.

The analysis was conducted using **Microsoft Excel**, with an interactive dashboard built using pivot tables, charts, and slicers to allow dynamic filtering by channel, time, and loan status.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

The project aims to answer key business questions:

* Where are users dropping off in the funnel?
* Which channels bring high-quality leads?
* How can conversion rates be improved?
* Which stages of the funnel require optimization?

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Techniques

* **Microsoft Excel**

  * Pivot Tables
  * Calculated Fields (Conversion Rate)
  * Interactive Slicers
  * Dashboard Design

---

## 📁 Dataset

The analysis is based on the **Bank Marketing Campaign Dataset**, which includes customer information, contact methods, and campaign outcomes.

---

## 📈 Key Analysis Performed

### 1. Conversion Analysis

* Calculated overall **conversion rate (12%)**
* Compared conversion performance across contact channels
* Identified consistency in conversion despite differences in lead volume

### 2. Funnel Performance

* Analyzed lead-to-customer journey
* Identified a **high drop-off rate (88%)**
* Highlighted inefficiencies in the final conversion stage

### 3. Channel Performance

* **Cellular** generated the highest number of leads
* Conversion rates were relatively similar across channels
* Indicates that lead quality is consistent, but conversion strategy may need improvement

### 4. Time-Based Trends

* Peak conversions observed in **May and June**
* Suggests possible campaign effectiveness or seasonal trends

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

* KPI cards (Total Leads, Total Customers, Conversion Rate, Drop-off Rate)
* Monthly conversion trend analysis
* Channel performance comparison
* Conversion funnel overview
* Interactive slicers:

  * Contact Type
  * Month
  * Housing Loan Status

---

## 🔑 Key Insights

* A significant portion of users drop off before conversion, indicating a gap in the **lead-to-customer stage**
* High lead volume does not necessarily translate into higher conversions
* Certain months outperform others, suggesting opportunities for **campaign optimization**

---

## 💡 Recommendations

* Improve **lead follow-up and nurturing strategies** to reduce drop-off
* Focus on optimizing the **final conversion stage**
* Analyze and replicate strategies used during **high-performing months (May–June)**
* Enhance targeting and engagement across all channels to improve efficiency
